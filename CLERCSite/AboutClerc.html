About CLERC and the LIONS Association

The Canadian Lions Eyeglass Recycling Centre (CLERC) is a non-profit organization affiliated with Lions Clubs International. CLERC's primary objective is to collect, sort, and recycle used eyeglasses. Through their efforts, CLERC aims to provide affordable eyewear to individuals in need, both in Canada and in developing countries. They collect donated eyeglasses from individuals, eye care professionals, and organizations, carefully sort them based on prescription and condition, and distribute them to partner organizations for redistribution. By recycling and repurposing used eyeglasses, CLERC contributes to improving vision and quality of life for those who lack access to proper eyewear. CLERC's work is vital in addressing the global issue of vision impairment, particularly among underserved communities. By collecting and recycling used eyeglasses, CLERC helps bridge the gap between the demand for eyewear and the lack of access to affordable options. Their efforts not only benefit individuals who require corrective lenses but also contribute to reducing waste and promoting sustainability. CLERC's collaboration with Lions Clubs and other partner organizations ensures that the recycled eyeglasses reach those who need them the most, making a tangible difference in people's lives. The success of CLERC relies on the dedication and commitment of their volunteers and the support of Lions Clubs and other stakeholders. Through their collective efforts, CLERC strives to create a positive impact by improving vision health and promoting access to eyewear for individuals who would otherwise face challenges due to financial constraints. By focusing on recycling and redistribution, CLERC's work exemplifies the power of community-driven initiatives in addressing critical healthcare needs and fostering inclusivity in vision care
